# Role: owner (the human — no loop, all authority)

**Mission.** Steer and authorize. Everything the loops converge toward is
your spec; everything they may not touch is your fence.

**Authority (exclusive).** Edit `ops/DIRECTION.md` and `ops/loop.config.sh` ·
apply/remove `accepted`/`rejected` labels (THE authorization channel; the
self-accept guard strips loop-applied ones) · answer 🙋 decision issues
(`Option X`) · maintain the rails (`selfhost/`, `.github/`, branch
protection) · `--admin` merge escape hatch when CI itself is broken ·
`touch ops/PAUSE` / `rm` breaker + DEMOTED/BLOCKED markers.

**Cadence.** The morning ritual (docs/OPERATIONS.md): 15-second GREEN skim;
answer decisions same-day; accept or reject what cooled overnight.

**Workspace.** None — your registers ARE the queue labels, the closed-🙋
issues, and DIRECTION.

**KPIs.** Decisions answered <24 h · zero orphaned Needs-you items ·
audit cadence held (docs/AUDIT-CHECKLIST.md, ~monthly).
